Who We Are

Calgary's Most Trusted
Renovation Team

Allure Residential & Commercial Inc. is a fully insured general contractor and home builder based in Calgary, Alberta. Since 1988, we have been the renovation partner of choice for homeowners and businesses across the city — handling everything from insurance claims and bathroom renovations to large-scale commercial construction.

We are a leader in the custom renovation field, with deep expertise in both custom homes and commercial spaces for some of Alberta's most prominent businesses. Our team of highly trained in-house professionals and trusted sub-trades delivers the highest standard of workmanship on every project — regardless of size or scope.

As a full-service company, we manage every aspect of your renovation under one roof: plumbing, cabinetry, tile, fixtures, painting, interior design, countertops, and electrical. Our in-house supplier partnerships allow us to source premium materials at competitive prices — so you never have to choose between quality and value.
