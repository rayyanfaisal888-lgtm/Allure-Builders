Who We Are

Calgary's Most Trusted
Renovation Team

Allure Residential & Commercial Inc. is a fully insured home builder and general contractor based in Calgary, Alberta. Since 1988, we have been the renovation partner of choice for homeowners and businesses across Wild Rose Country — handling everything from insurance claims and bathroom renovations to large-scale commercial construction and basement developments.

We are a leader in the custom renovation field, with deep expertise in both custom homes and commercial renovations for some of Alberta's most prominent companies. With over 30 years of hands-on experience, we have the knowledge and craftsmanship to bring any vision to life — whether it's a kitchen refresh or a full commercial buildout.

As a truly full-service company, we manage every aspect of your renovation under one roof: plumbing, cabinets, tile, fixtures, painting, interior decorating, countertops, and electrical. Our in-house supplier partnerships allow us to source premium materials at competitive prices — so you never have to choose between quality and value. All employees are fully bonded, carry full WCB coverage, and we are insured to $5 million liability on site.
